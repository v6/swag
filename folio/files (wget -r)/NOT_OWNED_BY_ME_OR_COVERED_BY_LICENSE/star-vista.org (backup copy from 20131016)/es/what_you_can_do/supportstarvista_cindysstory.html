"Cecilia", a graduate of one of our residential programs, now a Junior in college, has stayed in close contact with her Case Manager and with the host family we matched her with. She has dinner with them once a week, and knows that through our Transitional Housing programs she will always have a safe and welcoming place to stay between semesters and internships.

Please become a partner to a young adult like her by making a donation to ensure they have someone to rely on when they need help.
