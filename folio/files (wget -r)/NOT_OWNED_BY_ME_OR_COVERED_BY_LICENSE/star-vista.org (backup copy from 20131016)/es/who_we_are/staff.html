Our Staff
StarVista’s dedicated professional staff offer unparalleled prevention, early
intervention, education, and counseling resources and services to
more than 34,000 people throughout San Mateo County every year.

Management

Sara Larios Mitchell, Ph. D. , Chief Executive Officer
650-591-9623, ext. 112, smitchell@star-vista.org

Sara was appointed CEO of StarVista in October 2012, on the retirement of Michael Garb. She has a Master’s in Counseling Psychology from Golden Gate University, and a Doctorate in Transpersonal Psychology from the Institute of Transpersonal Psychology. With over 15 years of experience in clinical services, including early childhood, domestic violence, trauma, youth development, family support and counseling, Sara is strongly committed to the children, families and individuals of our diverse community, and to strengthening opportunities for everyone to transform their lives.

Dianette Washer, Chief Financial Officer
650-591-9623 ext. 113, dwasher@star-vista.org

Dianette has worked in the non-profit sector in San Mateo County for over 20
years, with various programs and agencies that are now part of StarVista. Prior to joining FACES, Inc. in 1997, Dianette served as the Director of Finance and Administration at the Suicide Prevention and Crisis Center in Burlingame for 12 years. A native of Denmark, Dianette earned a B.S. in Accounting from Hayward State University.

Douglas Styles, PsyD, Associate Director
650-591-9623 ext. 115, douglas.styles@star-vista.org

Douglas joined StarVista (then YFES) in January, 2004, as the Director of the Department of Crisis, Youth, and Family Services. He came to the organization
from River Oak Center for Children in Sacramento, and has extensive
experience supervising clinical staff, providing therapy, and
directing the administrative operations of programs serving high-need
families. Douglas is a Licensed Psychologist, a Licensed Marriage and
Family Therapist, and a Registered Drama Therapist. He received his
Doctor of Psychology in Clinical Psychology from the California
School of Professional Psychology and is an Adjunct Faculty member of
Capella University.

Clarise Blanchard, Ph.D., Director, Department of Health and Recovery Services
650-591-3739 ext. 151, cblanchard@star-vista.org

Clarise is a licensed Marriage and Family Therapist who joined StarVista (then FACES) as Program Manager of the Women's Enrichment Center in August, 2000.
Prior to that, Clarise served as a Supervisor at Pyramid Alternatives
in Pacifica for 10 years, where she worked with adults, children and
youth, and provided counseling for individuals struggling with
addictions, mental health issues and domestic violence. A graduate of
the University of California, Berkeley with a B.A. in Economics,
Clarise also earned an M.A. in Counseling and Guidance from the
University of Reno, and a Ph.D. in Counseling Psychology from the
Northern California Graduate University.

Sarah Poulain, M.A., M.F.T., Interim Director, Department of Early Childhood and Family Services
650-344-3909 ext. 17, sarah.poulain@star-vista.org

Sarah Poulain received her Masters Degree in Marriage and Family Therapy in 2005 and has worked as a family counselor for families in crisis for many years. She began at StarVista in December 2005 as a Mental Health Clinician in the Healthy Homes Program, became the Program Coordinator of the Learning Together program in 2007, and was promoted to Program Manager of the Learning Together Program in 2010.

Judith Jones, M.A, Development Director
650-591-9623 ext. 103, judith.jones@star-vista.org

A native of Great Britain, Judith earned her M.A. at Oxford University
and worked as a General Manager in London’s National Health Service
before coming to California and starting a career in the Bay Area
nonprofit sector. Judith joined the Development Department at
StarVista (then YFES) in August 2005. She has worked as the Executive Director of
the Community Breast Health Project (now Breast Cancer Connections)
in Palo Alto, and as a consultant to a variety of local nonprofits.
