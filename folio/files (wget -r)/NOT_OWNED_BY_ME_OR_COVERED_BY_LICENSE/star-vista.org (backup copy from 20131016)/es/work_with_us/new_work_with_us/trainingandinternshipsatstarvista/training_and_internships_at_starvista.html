Training and Internships

As a member of the California Psychology Internship Council (CAPIC), StarVista provides professional training to psychology practicum students, marriage and family therapist trainees/interns, social work interns, associate clinical social workers, and pre-doctoral psychology interns. StarVista’s professional staff includes psychologists, social workers, marriage and family therapists and highly trained paraprofessionals in the areas of child development and substance abuse treatment. Supervision is provided primarily by licensed psychologists (Psy.D. or Ph.D. in Clinical, Counseling, or Educational Psychology), licensed clinical social workers (LCSW), and licensed marriage and family therapists (MFT).

StarVista provides high-quality professional training and supervised experience that will afford each clinician-in-training the opportunity to build cultural, ethical, and clinical competence in the assessment and treatment of children, adolescents, adults, and their families. Weekly in-service training seminars are provided throughout the training year, and we also offer a “live” therapy supervision/training seminar experience. Our state-of-the-art facility enables a trainee’s therapy session to be observed and videotaped from behind a one-way mirror. The trainee is able to wear an earpiece to receive in-the-moment supervision, and trainees are able to analyze and discuss the session in subsequent process groups.
