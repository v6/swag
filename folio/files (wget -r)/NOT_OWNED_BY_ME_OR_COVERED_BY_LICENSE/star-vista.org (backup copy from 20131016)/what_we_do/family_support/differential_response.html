Differential Response

This is a voluntary prevention program for families with children aged 0 - 5. We connect families to resources within their own community, help them discover individual and collective strengths and empower them to make positive and proactive changes for their family's future.

Low to moderate risk families are referred to our program through San Mateo County Child Protective Services, and receive home-based case management services including:

  • Home and community visits

  • Familly assessment

  • Collaborative goal setting

  • Support plan
