Fabritec Wall Panels, made from Class 1 fire-rated willtec, provide sound absorption comparable to fiberglass panels.

With a custom look, durable construction, and excellent sound-absorbing characteristics, Fabritec Wall Panels easily add style and performance to schools, religious facilities, gymnasiums, restaurants, offices, auditoriums, lobbies, and other noisy open areas.

Available in custom and standard sizes, Fabritec panels create a design that perfectly fits your space. The rugged and tackable exterior of Fabritec panels can be covered with Guilford or other fabrics. Guilford fabric selections include a wide range of current colors and textures to complement, or blend with, any decor.

Fabritec Wall Panels are just as easy to install as they are to specify. Built from a unique willtec foam core, they offer outstanding acoustical performance, absorbing up to 85% of the sound directly at them.

A lightweight 1/16th fiberglass substrate makes Fabritec Wall Panels tackable and impact resistant. Fabritec Wall Panels are lightweight, making them easy to install with adhesive and/or hook and loop.
