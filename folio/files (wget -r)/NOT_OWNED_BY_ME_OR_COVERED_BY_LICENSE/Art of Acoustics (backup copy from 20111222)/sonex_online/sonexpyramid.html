Sonex Pyramid Panels offer outstanding acoustic control across all frequencies with Noise Reduction Coefficient (NRC) ranging from 0.75 to 1.05. The unique geometric design of these panels dramatically increases the panels' surface area, providing much more acoustic control than flat surfaces.

Made of a Class 1 fire-rated willtec foam, Sonex Pyramid panels are available in natural, painted, or HPC finishes. HPC protects the foam from dust and dirt and makes it possible to wipe panels down.

Sonex Pyramid panels are easy to install on any wall and/or ceiling surface with PA-02 adhesive. Can be cut using a sharp knife.
