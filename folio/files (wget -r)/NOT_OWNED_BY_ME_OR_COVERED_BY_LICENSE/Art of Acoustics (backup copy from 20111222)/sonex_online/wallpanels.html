Sonex One Panels

Sonex Valueline Panels




The sculpted surface of each Sonex One panel deflects and disperses sound waves, allowing the open cell willtec foam to convert sound energy into quiet, kinetic energy.




Sonex Valueline Panels provide effective acoustic control at an affordable price. The subtle surface pattern on these panels render them inconspicuous in most settings.






Sonex Pyramid Panels

Sonex Classic Panels




The unique geometric design of these Sonex Pyramid panels dramatically increases the panels' surface area, providing much more acoustic control than flat surfaces.



Sonex Classic Panels feature a modified anechoic wedge design for cost-effective, yet impressive sound control. Sonex Classics offer optimum absorption and are ideal for sound studios.
