Flat Acoustical Foam Panels

Sonex Flat panels offer all the benefits of acoustical foam panels, but with a flat face. These panels can be applied directly to walls and ceilings, as well as used inside HVAC systems, to help quiet sound reverberation.

Sonex Flat panels are made of Class 1 fire-rated willtec foam.

Sonex Flat panels are easy to install on any wall and/or ceiling surface with PA-02 adhesive. Installers can attach them in HVAC systems using general HVAC mounting clips.

These panels are easily cut in the field using a sharp utility knife.
