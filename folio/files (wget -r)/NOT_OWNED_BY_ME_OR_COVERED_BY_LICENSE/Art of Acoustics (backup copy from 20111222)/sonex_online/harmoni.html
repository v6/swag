Harmoni ceiling tiles are built from lightweight willtec foam, making for easy installation and excellent sound absorption across all frequencies. The tiles are available in two styles, both with a tegular edge. The Vista pattern has a smooth surface for a clean look while the Tartan pattern provides a softly sculpted style.

Made for ceiling grid applications, Harmoni ceiling panels provide excellent sound absorption and have Noise Reduction Coefficients (NRC) up to 0.90.

Made of Class 1 fire-rated willtec foam, Harmoni panels are available in arctic white, black onyx, grey mist, and almond Hypalon paint. Harmoni panels maintain their integrity in moist and humid environments without sagging.
