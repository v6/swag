Sonex Clean products are designed for environments that require noise control with durable, washable and cleanable materials. The baffles, panels and ceiling tiles are ideal for use in a variety of different clean room applications. Sonex Clean products meet USDA/FDA requirements.

Sonex Clean products are fully encapsulated in FR taffeta vinyl for efficient cleaning and long, durable life. They offer excellent sound absorption to reduce reverberation or echo.

Sonex Clean products are made from a Class 1 fire-rated willtec foam. Sonex Clean is available in 17 standard colors.
