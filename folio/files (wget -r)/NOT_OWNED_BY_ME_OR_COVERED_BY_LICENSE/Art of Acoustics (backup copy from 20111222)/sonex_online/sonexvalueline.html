Sonex Valueline Panels offer excellent acoustical control across all frequencies with Noise Reduction Coefficients (NRC) ranging from 0.75 to 1.05. They are especially effective at absorbing excessive sound at the middle frequencies (500 and 1,000 Hz) where unwanted noise and reverberation can interfere with communication. P

Made of a Class 1 fire-rated willtec foam, Sonex Valueline panels are available in natural, painted, or HPC finishes. HPC coats and protects the foam from dust and dirt and makes it possible to wipe panels down.

Sonex Valueline panels are easy to install on any wall and/or ceiling surface with PA-02 adhesive. Can be cut using a sharp knife.
