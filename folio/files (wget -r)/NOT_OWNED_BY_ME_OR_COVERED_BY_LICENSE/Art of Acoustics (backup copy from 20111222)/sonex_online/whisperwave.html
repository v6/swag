Whisperwave panels are made of an Class 1 fire-rated willtec foam. Panels can be painted or coated with HPC.

Whisperwave panels are easy to install in the field using the included corkscrew hangers. Can be aligned in the same direction or arranged so that every other baffle is turned 90 degrees.
