About Us

West General is an acoustical and environmental systems distributor. We want to make it easy to find the right acoustical products quickly. If desired, our team has the experience and resources to deliver a custom solution to your acoustical problem.

Our goal is to treat all of our customers, exactly the way we would expect to be treated: fairly and honestly.

Though we have only recently moved into the business of online selling through store.westgeneral.com, our company has proved its worth to our customers time and time again since 1975.

We maintain the following standards:

  • Rapid quote response and follow up on all sales
  • Knowledgeable sales force
  • Cost competitive, high-quality systems and materials

Any time you need something beyond the usual, or have a specific budget or time frame, please give us a call at 800.801.9378 - If we cannot help you, we will refer you to an acoustic consultant who can - We always try to work with our customers, and have likely dealt successfully with the same situation in the past.

Thank you.
