Product Description

Sonex One panels are attractive and reliable, featuring Noise Reduction Coefficient(NRC) ranging from 0.85 to 1.10. The sculpted surface of each panel absorbs sound waves, allowing open-cell pores in the willtec foam to convert sound energy into quiet, kinetic energy. Each panel has a smooth bevel around all four edges for a uniform appearance.

Made of an Class 1 fire-rated willtec foam Sonex One panels are available in natural, or HPC finishes. The HPC finish coats and protects the foam from dust and dirt and makes it possible to wipe panels down with a moist cloth.

Sonex One panels are easy to install on any wall and/or ceiling surface with PA-02 adhesive. Can be cut using a sharp knife.
