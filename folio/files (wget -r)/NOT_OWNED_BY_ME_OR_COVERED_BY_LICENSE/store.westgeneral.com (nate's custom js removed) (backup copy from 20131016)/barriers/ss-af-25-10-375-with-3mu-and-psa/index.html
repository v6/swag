Product Description

SS-AF-25-10-375 WITH 3MU AND PSA is a custom composite used to quiet server and other computer based cabinets. This product greatly reduces noise levels from server components, while not significantly altering the enclosures airflow. The products 5/8" thickness makes it thin enough to be installed into tight spaces.

Custom composite, consisting of the following components bonded together:

PSA pressure sensitive adhesive
1/4" thick polyurethane foam acoustical decoupler
1/8" thick 1 pound per square foot vinyl noise barrier
3/8" thick polyurethane foam noise absorber
3 mil urethane facing, color black

This product comes in rolls measuring 54" x 25'.
