FAQ

Question : Do you ship out of the United States?

Answer : Yes, we can arrange shipment outside the United States. Please call 408-255-8644 for shipment options and more details.

Question : Do you accept purchase orders?

Answer : Yes, we do, subject to credit approval. Just send your purchase order or RFQ to wgl@westgeneral.com for priority attention. Please advise your desired shipping method and account number.

Question : How long does it take for my order to arrive?

Answer : Many products are manufactured or coated to order, which can take between one to three weeks. After that, UPS or Common Carrier shipping usually takes between 3-6 business days.

Question : Can I get a discount on shipping?

Answer : Please contact us for orders over 8 boxes, it is frequently cheaper to get a quotation for LTL freight from a common carrier shipping company. Just give us a call or send us a request. We will do the legwork for you, and find the cheapest freight quote available for your order.

Question : What is an “NRC?”

Answer : This is the “noise reduction coefficient,” an ANSI standard which roughly gives an idea of how to compare the noise absorption of a similar area of different acoustic material.

Question : Do you accept returns?

Answer : See our return policy for details, or give us a call.
